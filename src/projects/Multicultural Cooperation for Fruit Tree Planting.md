title: Multicultural Cooperation for Fruit Tree Planting
organization: High Atlas Foundation
date: 2019-11-12
category: Community Development
tags: ['Economic', 'Multicultural']
region: ['Morocco']
image: ./images/fruit_tree_multicultural.jpg
supporters: 450
intro: For 5 years, the Jewish Community of Marrakech will offer the use of arable land for HAF to build a nursery on which to raise 80-120,000 fruit tree saplings and train village representatives in arboriculture. In the 2nd and 5th years, the trees will be donated to each household of the surrounding disadvantaged villages and by 6-8 years later, the fruit yields will at least double the income of those rural communities, economically and environmentally benefiting 10,000 people.
